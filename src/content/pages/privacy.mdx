# Privacy Policy

_Last updated: August 15, 2025 · Version 1.0_

Welcome to Adol's Blog ("we", "our" or "us"). This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you visit **adol.tech** (the "Blog") or interact with our content.

## 1. Information we collect

1. **Information you provide voluntarily.** If you contact us via email or comment forms, we may collect your name, email address, and message content.
2. **Information collected automatically.** IP address, browser type, device information, pages visited, and time spent on the blog (via cookies and analytics).
3. **No third-party data collection.** We do not purchase or collect data from third-party sources.

## 2. How we use your information

• Operate and maintain the blog.  
• Respond to your inquiries and comments.  
• Analyze blog traffic and improve content.  
• Send occasional updates about new posts (only with your explicit consent).  
• Ensure the security and proper functioning of the blog.  
• Comply with legal obligations when required.

## 3. Legal basis for processing (GDPR)

Our processing is based on: (i) legitimate interests in operating the blog and improving content, (ii) your consent for marketing communications, and (iii) compliance with legal obligations.

## 4. Sharing your information

**We do not sell or rent your personal data.** We may share information only with:  
• **GitHub Pages** (hosting provider) for blog hosting.  
• **Analytics providers** (e.g., Google Analytics) to understand blog traffic.  
• **Legal authorities** when required by law or to protect rights and safety.

## 5. Data transfers

This blog is hosted on GitHub Pages. Your data may be processed in countries outside your residence, including the United States. We rely on GitHub's privacy safeguards and policies.

## 6. Data retention

We retain analytics data for up to 26 months. Contact information is retained only as long as necessary to respond to your inquiries or until you request deletion.

## 7. Your rights

You have the right to:  
• **Access** your personal data  
• **Correct** inaccurate information  
• **Delete** your data (right to be forgotten)  
• **Restrict** processing  
• **Object** to processing based on legitimate interests  
• **Data portability** (receive your data in a structured format)  
• **Withdraw consent** at any time

## 8. Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information, including secure hosting via GitHub Pages and encrypted data transmission.

## 9. Children's privacy

This blog is not directed at children under 16. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## 10. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

## 11. Contact us

For questions about this Privacy Policy or to exercise your rights, please contact us at:  
**Email:** adol@adol.tech
