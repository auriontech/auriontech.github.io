# Cookies Policy

_Last updated: August 15, 2025 · Version 1.0_

Adol's Blog ("we", "our" or "us") uses cookies and similar technologies when you visit **adol.tech** (the "Blog"). This Cookies Policy explains what these technologies are, why we use them, and how you can control them.

## 1. What are cookies?

Cookies are small data files placed on your device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work, improve efficiency, and provide reporting information.

## 2. Types of cookies we use

| Type        | Purpose                                       | Examples (lifespan)               |
| ----------- | --------------------------------------------- | --------------------------------- |
| Essential   | Necessary for the blog to function properly.  | Cookie consent (1 year)           |
| Performance | Help us understand how visitors use the blog. | Google Analytics (_ga – 2 years_) |
| Functional  | Remember your preferences (theme, language).  | Theme preference (persistent)     |

**Note:** We do **not** use advertising or marketing cookies on this personal blog.

## 3. How can you control cookies?

You have several options to manage cookies:

• **Cookie consent banner:** Use our cookie consent dialog to accept or reject non-essential cookies.  
• **Browser settings:** Configure your browser to block or delete cookies (may affect blog functionality).  
• **Analytics opt-out:** Visit [Google Analytics Opt-out](https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout) to prevent analytics tracking.

## 4. Other tracking technologies

We may use local storage to remember your theme preferences and other functional settings.

## 5. Third-party services

This blog may use Google Analytics to understand visitor patterns. Google has its own cookie policy which you can review on their website.

## 6. Changes to this policy

We may update this Cookies Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

## 7. Contact us

If you have questions about our use of cookies, please contact us through our [Privacy Policy](/privacy) contact information.
